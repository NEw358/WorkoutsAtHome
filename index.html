What is or purpouse

And why do we do it


We are aiming to help people of all ages workout more easily at home. We have a big collection of exercises for the domestic enviroment. There are plenty of options for both male and female users. There are a lot of weightless exercises and some with weights, obtainable at home. Yoga poses and stances are also available. There are explanations on how to do it for everything. The biggest part of all is the custom timer. Organize your workout and let us help you complete it. All you have to do is choose from different options or use the ones we suggest.


While going to the gyn is amazing, some of us either don't have the time for it or don't find the need for it. Staying healthy is very important and exercise is a way of achieving it. You can easily create a workout to do for ~10 minutes in the morning and then go about your day. You can create something more complex or even sign up for one of our challenges. Whatever you need we have an easy solution for you.
